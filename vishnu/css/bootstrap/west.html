Many of our components require the use of JavaScript to function. Specifically, they require jQuery, Popper, and our own JavaScript plugins. We use jQuerys slim build, but the full version is also supported.Place one of the following near the end of your pages, right before the closing tag, to enable them. jQuery must come first, then Popper, and then our JavaScript plugins.
